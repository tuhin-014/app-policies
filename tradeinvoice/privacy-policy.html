TradeInvoice — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

TradeInvoice is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal or business information.

Data Collection

We collect no data. All information you enter — invoices, client details, expenses, business profile — is stored exclusively on your device.

No Network Access

TradeInvoice does not connect to the internet.

Camera & Microphone

TradeInvoice requests camera access to scan receipts and microphone access for voice input. All processing happens on-device. No data is transmitted.

No Third-Party Services

No third-party SDKs, analytics, advertising, or tracking.

Data Deletion

All data is stored locally. Uninstalling removes all data permanently.
