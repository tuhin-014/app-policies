ClearBill — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

Overview

ClearBill ("the App") is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal or medical billing information.

Data Collection

We collect no data. All information you enter into ClearBill — including medical bills, insurance details, personal information for dispute letters, and savings records — is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's on-device database technology (SwiftData).

No Network Access

ClearBill does not connect to the internet. The app requests no network permissions and makes no API calls, analytics requests, or telemetry transmissions of any kind.

No Third-Party Services

ClearBill contains no third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

Notifications

ClearBill may request permission to send local notifications for bill payment reminders and dispute follow-ups. These notifications are scheduled entirely on your device and do not involve any server communication.

Data Storage & Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time through the app's Settings screen. Uninstalling the app removes all associated data permanently.

Children's Privacy

ClearBill is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
