DepositGuard - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Overview

DepositGuard ("we", "our", or "the app") is a rental deposit protection app published by VertexLab Solutions. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. DepositGuard is designed to be completely private:

  • All data (property information, photos, documents, maintenance records) is stored exclusively on your device.
  • No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.
  • No account or registration is required to use the app.
  • No analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs are included.
  • No cookies or similar technologies are used.

Device Permissions

DepositGuard may request the following device permissions:

  • Camera: To take photos of your rental property for documentation purposes. Photos are stored only on your device.
  • Photo Library: To access existing photos for property documentation. Photos remain on your device.
  • Location: To optionally tag photos with GPS coordinates for evidence purposes. Location data is stored only on your device.
  • Notifications: To send optional reminders about lease dates and maintenance tasks. All notification data stays on your device.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using the device's built-in database and file storage. This includes:

  • Property information and lease details
  • Photos and documentation
  • Maintenance records
  • Dispute letter drafts
  • App settings and preferences

If you delete the app, all data will be permanently removed from your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not have access to your data. When you use the app's sharing features (e.g., exporting a PDF report or sharing milestone cards), you control what is shared and with whom.

Children's Privacy

DepositGuard is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
