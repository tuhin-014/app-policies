MealRx — Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 9, 2026

Developer: Vertex Lab Solutions

Overview

MealRx is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal health data. All your information stays on your device.

Data Collection

MealRx does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account creation or sign-up required
  • No personal health information is transmitted to servers
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad-related data collection
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data
  • Health conditions and meal preferences are stored only on your device

Health Data

MealRx allows you to input health conditions to generate personalized meal plans. This information is:

  • Stored only on your device using local storage
  • Never transmitted to any external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Deleted when you uninstall the app

Camera Access

MealRx may request camera access for barcode scanning. Camera data is:

  • Processed entirely on your device
  • Used only for reading product barcodes
  • Never stored, recorded, or transmitted

In-App Purchases

MealRx offers optional subscription purchases through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

MealRx is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Third-Party Services

MealRx does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. All meal plan generation is performed on-device.

Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete all app data by uninstalling MealRx.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
