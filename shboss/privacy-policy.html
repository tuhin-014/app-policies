SideHustle Boss

Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

Overview

SideHustle Boss ("the App") is built with privacy as a core principle. We collect zero data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers. All data — including earnings, expenses, mileage, and tax calculations — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

Camera & Photo Library

The App may request access to your camera and photo library for capturing receipt photos. These photos are stored locally on your device only. They are never uploaded or transmitted.

Location Services

The App may request location access for GPS mileage tracking. Location data is processed on-device to calculate trip distances. It is never transmitted to external servers.

Analytics & Tracking

The App contains no analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. We do not use cookies, device fingerprinting, or any form of user tracking.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases. Apple's privacy practices apply to purchase transactions. No other third-party services are used.

Data Export

You can export your data as JSON at any time through the app's Settings. You can also delete all data from within the app.

Financial Disclaimer

Tax calculations are estimates based on publicly available tax rates and formulas. They are not financial advice. Consult a qualified tax professional for your specific situation.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.
