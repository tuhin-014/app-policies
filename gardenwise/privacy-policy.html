🌿 GardenWise — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 26, 2026

VertexLab Solutions ("we", "our", or "us") operates the GardenWise mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

GardenWise stores all your data locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

  • Plant profiles (names, photos, care schedules)
  • Scan results and images
  • Reminder settings
  • App preferences

We do not have access to this data. It remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Camera

GardenWise requests camera access to scan plant leaves, soil, and pests. Photos taken within the app are processed on-device using Apple's CoreML framework and are not uploaded to external servers unless you are using the Premium AI scanning feature.

Location

GardenWise requests location access to determine your USDA growing zone for seasonal planting recommendations. Your location is used locally and is not stored or transmitted to our servers.

Premium AI Scanning

If you subscribe to GardenWise Pro and use the advanced AI scanning feature, plant images may be sent to our third-party AI provider (Kindwise/Plant.id) for analysis. These images are:

  • Used solely for plant health analysis
  • Not linked to your identity
  • Not stored permanently by the AI provider
  • Processed in accordance with the provider's privacy policy

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identification information
  • We do not track your location history
  • We do not use analytics or tracking SDKs
  • We do not sell any data to third parties
  • We do not use advertising identifiers

Subscriptions

GardenWise Pro subscriptions are processed through Apple's App Store. Payment information is handled entirely by Apple and is not accessible to us.

Children's Privacy

GardenWise does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is rated 4+ and is suitable for all ages.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data by deleting the GardenWise app from your device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and on this page.
