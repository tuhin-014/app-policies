RushOut — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 18, 2026

Overview

RushOut ("the App") is a mobile racing game. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection

RushOut does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data you enter — including game progress, high scores, and vehicle unlock status — is stored exclusively on your device. No data is sent to us, third parties, or any remote server.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using the device's built-in database. This data is not shared with any other applications or services.

Third-Party Services

RushOut does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Permissions

    RushOut does not require any special permissions.

All permissions are optional and can be managed through your device settings.

Children's Privacy

RushOut is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by using "Reset Data" in Settings, or by uninstalling the App.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
