QuitTrack — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Developer: VertexLab Solutions

Overview

QuitTrack is designed with your privacy as a top priority. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All your information stays on your device.

Data Collection

QuitTrack does not collect any personal data. Specifically:

  • No account creation or sign-up required
  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or ad-related data collection
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

Data Storage

All data you enter into QuitTrack (quit records, check-ins, cravings, motivations, settings) is stored locally on your device only. This data is not accessible to us or any third party.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data. When you use the share feature to create milestone cards, you control what is shared and with whom.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time by deleting individual quit records within the app, or by uninstalling the app, which removes all associated data from your device.

Children's Privacy

QuitTrack does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not collect information from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
