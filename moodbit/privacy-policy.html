MoodBit — Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Overview

MoodBit ("the App") is developed by VertexLab Solutions. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. MoodBit stores all information locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Mood entries (score, tags, notes, timestamps)
  • Habit definitions and daily completion logs
  • Correlation calculations
  • App preferences (dark mode, reminder settings)

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

No Analytics or Tracking

MoodBit does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track your usage, behavior, or any other activity.

No Account Required

MoodBit does not require you to create an account, sign in, or provide any personal information to use the App.

Data Export

You may export your data as a CSV file using the built-in export feature. This file is generated locally and shared via your device's share sheet. We do not have access to exported files.

Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time using the "Clear All Data" option in Settings. Uninstalling the App also removes all stored data.

Notifications

MoodBit may send local notifications for mood and habit reminders if you enable them. These notifications are scheduled locally on your device and do not involve any server communication.

Children's Privacy

MoodBit does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App does not collect any data from any user.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
